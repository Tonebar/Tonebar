Three pieces, one connection

Each part runs where it needs to — nothing to configure beyond entering a code once.

01

Install the bridge

A small menu-bar app for your Mac or PC. It sits quietly and connects your phone to Lightroom — no terminal, no setup screens.

02

Add the Lightroom plugin

One-time step through Lightroom's own Plug-in Manager. The bridge app can open the folder for you.

03

Pair your phone

Open the app, enter the code shown in your menu bar once, and it's remembered from then on.
